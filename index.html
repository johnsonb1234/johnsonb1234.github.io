About LondrCoin

Real Utility.
Real World.
Real Coin.

$LONDR is the native utility token of the Londr ecosystem, a decentralized bridge between the Web3 economy and real-world gig services. Built on Ethereum and launched directly on Uniswap with no presale and no private allocations.

The token enables payments for gig services like laundry pickup and delivery, powers community rewards, and provides liquidity for an open, permissionless ecosystem. Every holder participates on equal footing from day one.

FAQ

Common Questions

Was there an ICO or IEO?▼
No. LondrCoin launched directly on Uniswap with no Initial Coin Offering (ICO) or Initial Exchange Offering (IEO) of any kind. The token has been available on the open market from the very first moment.
Were there any private or seed allocations?▼
Absolutely none. There are zero private sale allocations, seed rounds, or discounted distributions. Every single token is available to the public on equal terms through Uniswap.
What does $LONDR actually do?▼
$LONDR is a utility token that powers the Londr gig economy ecosystem. Holders can use it to pay for services like laundry pickup and delivery, earn community rewards, and participate in the growth of the Londr platform as it expands.
How do I buy $LONDR?▼
Go to Uniswap (app.uniswap.org), connect your Ethereum wallet (MetaMask, Rainbow, Coinbase Wallet, etc.), and swap ETH for $LONDR using the contract address: 0x97d1A1b493D83A35A30d7D868e0e1D821F15D424. Always verify the contract address before trading.
Which wallet should I use?▼
Any ERC-20 compatible Ethereum wallet works: MetaMask, Rainbow Wallet, Coinbase Wallet, Trust Wallet, or hardware wallets like Ledger. Make sure you have ETH for gas fees and the swap itself.
Is LondrCoin audited?▼
The smart contract is publicly verifiable on Etherscan. We encourage all community members to review the code. Formal third-party audit details will be shared via official channels. Follow our Telegram and GitHub for the latest updates.
